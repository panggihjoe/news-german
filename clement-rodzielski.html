Clement rodzielski. Galerie chantal crousel is pleased to present the fourth solo exhibition of the artist clement rodzielski from march 7 to may 23 2020. Clement rodzielskis mercurial practice revolves around imagesthe processes of their construction reproduction and circulation. Rodzielski applies paint to materials ranging from stacks of printer paper to magazine covers to alter each materials subjectivity or trace its movement. According to principles of duplication and re presentation of the images used the artist poses a reflection of the tumult of images from visual communication.

View more works by this artist. Whether drawn from archives contemporary media the internet or personally created the images in rodzielskis work undergo diverse processes ranging from multiplication repetition and reframing to collage. Clement rodzielski queries the original meaning of images through their channels of distribution and reproduction.
